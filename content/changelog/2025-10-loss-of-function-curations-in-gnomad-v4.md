---
title: Loss-of-Function Curations in gnomAD v4
date: 2025-10-03
order: 1
---

The gnomAD v4 dataset now includes loss-of-function (LoF) curations as part of the variant data viewable on the gene, region, and variant pages. We will continue to update the gnomAD browser as additional v4 curations are performed.

See the [data page](https://gnomad.broadinstitute.org/data#v4-lof-curation-results) to download the raw data, and the [help page](/help/lof-curation) and [blog post](https://gnomad.broadinstitute.org/news/2020-10-loss-of-function-curations-in-gnomad/) on loss-of-function curations for additional details.
