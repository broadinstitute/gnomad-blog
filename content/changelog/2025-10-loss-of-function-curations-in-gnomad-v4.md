---
title: Loss-of-Function Curations in gnomAD v4
date: 2025-10-03
order: 1
---

The gnomAD v4 dataset now includes loss of function (LoF) curations as part of the variant data viewable on the gene, region and variant pages. As additional v4 curations are performed, we will continue to update the gnomAD browser with these curations.

See the [data page](https://gnomad.broadinstitute.org/data#v4-lof-curation-results) to download the raw data, and our [full blog post](/help/lof-curation) on loss-of-function curations for additional details.
